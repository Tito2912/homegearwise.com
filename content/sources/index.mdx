    <h1>Sources &amp; verification</h1>
    <p>We prioritize primary sources whenever possible and encourage readers to verify key constraints before buying.</p>

  <section class="stack" aria-labelledby="primary">
    <h2 id="primary">Primary sources we rely on</h2>
    <div class="card">
      <ul>
        <li>Official product manuals (maintenance, safety, cleaning steps).</li>
        <li>Manufacturer spec sheets (dimensions, power, capacity, supported features).</li>
        <li>Official support docs and FAQs (warranty, parts, troubleshooting).</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </section>

  <section class="stack" aria-labelledby="verify">
    <h2 id="verify">Quick verification checklist</h2>
    <div class="card">
      <ul>
        <li>Measure your space (height under furniture, countertop, cabinet clearance).</li>
        <li>Check consumables and maintenance frequency (filters, descaling, bags).</li>
        <li>Confirm what is included vs optional accessories.</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </section>
