      <h2>MopExtend: edge mopping explained</h2>
      <p>
        MopExtend is the Dreame L10s Ultra's answer to a real limitation in most combo robots: mop pads that
        never quite reach baseboards and wall edges.
      </p>
      <div className="card">
        <ul>
          <li><strong>How it works:</strong> when the robot detects a wall or baseboard, the mop modules physically extend outward—getting the spinning pads closer to the edge than a fixed-width design allows.</li>
          <li><strong>Result:</strong> better coverage along walls, cabinets, and furniture bases compared to robots with fixed mop positions.</li>
          <li><strong>Limitation:</strong> MopExtend reaches closer, but does not fully clean inside corners—a quick manual pass on corners is still worthwhile weekly.</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <p className="muted">
