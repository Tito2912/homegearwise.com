    <section id="wifi-caveat">
      <h2>The 2.4 GHz Wi-Fi caveat</h2>
      <p>
        This is the most common friction point reported for the QV 35A. The robot requires a 2.4 GHz Wi-Fi connection
        during setup and for ongoing app control.
      </p>
      <div className="card">
        <ul>
          <li>If your router broadcasts both 2.4 GHz and 5 GHz on the <strong>same network name</strong>, your phone may connect to 5 GHz automatically during setup—and the robot will fail to pair.</li>
          <li><strong>Fix:</strong> temporarily separate the two bands in your router settings (give 2.4 GHz its own name), complete setup, then restore your settings.</li>
          <li>If your router is 5 GHz only, this robot is not compatible without a separate access point or router that supports 2.4 GHz.</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <p className="muted">
        Check your router's admin panel before purchasing if you are unsure which bands it broadcasts.
      </p>
