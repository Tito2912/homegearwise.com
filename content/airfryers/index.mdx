          <h2 id="faq-title">FAQ</h2>
          <div class="stack">
            <div class="card">
              <h3>Is the vertical DoubleStack “better”?</h3>
              <p>
                It’s better if your main constraint is <strong>counter width</strong>. If you have room, the side-by-side Foodi
                MAX may feel simpler day to day because both baskets are at the same height.
              </p>
            </div>
            <div class="card">
              <h3>Are they big enough for a family?</h3>
              <p>
                Often yes, but “family size” depends on your portions and what you cook. Confirm the basket volume and usable
                cooking area on the listing.
              </p>
            </div>
            <div class="card">
              <h3>What’s the #1 thing to check?</h3>
              <p>
                <strong>Basket shape + accessories.</strong> Two models can share the same total liters/quarts and still cook very
                differently depending on depth, width, and included racks.
              </p>
            </div>
            <div class="card">
              <h3>Do I need a smart thermometer?</h3>
              <p>
                Only if you’ll use it. It’s great for meats, but many people never plug it in. If precision matters to you,
                choose a bundle that includes it and confirm compatibility.
              </p>
            </div>
